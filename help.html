Help

What is the role of the QA person in the NCR website?

The QA person is responsible for filling out the QA part of the form and sending it to the engineering department. They can also add suppliers to the system.

How does the QA person notify the engineering department after completing their part?

Once the QA person completes their part and sends the form, both the QA and engineering departments receive email notifications.

What can the engineering person do with the form?

The engineering person can fill out their part of the form and view the QA part, but they cannot edit the QA part.

How does the engineering person notify the purchasing department after completing their part?

After completing their part, the engineering person sends the form, and both the engineering and purchasing departments receive email notifications.

What is the role of the purchasing department in the NCR website?

The purchasing department is responsible for filling out their part of the form and submitting it after receiving a notification from the engineering department.

Who has the authority to edit the form for all departments?

Only the admin has the authority to edit the form for all departments.

Can the admin add suppliers and employees to the system?

Yes, the admin can add both suppliers and employees to the system.

Can the QA person add suppliers to the system?

Yes, the QA person can also add suppliers to the system.

What happens after the purchasing department submits their part?

The process is complete, and the admin reviews the form for accuracy and final approval.

What should I do if I encounter an issue with the form?

If you encounter any issues with the form, please contact the admin for assistance.
